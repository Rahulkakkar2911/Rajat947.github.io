About us

We glad you come here to read about us

Love traditional Indian food? So do we! And it is this authentic, delicious and subtly aromatic food we offer. Our trained and experienced chef, Anand Kumar, has worked with some of the most famous and creative restaurants in the world. Trained as a culinary chef in India for 10 years, Chef Kumar brings a tantalizing array of dishes with the perfect aroma and taste to satisfy anyone’s pallette.

Where It all Begin !

When you enter my restaurant, you will find a clean and friendly atmosphere, and me, your Chef, walking the floor greeting my guests and answering any question you may have.

Being a service oriented Chef, I absolutely love meeting and getting to know you. I have over 20 years experience in providing authentic Indian and Indo-Chinese cuisine. For 10 years, I learned from the great Chefs of India. I studied and absorbed as much as I could. In 1997 I graduated from the culinary school in Lucknow (formerly known as Awadh in the old days), specializing in Indian and Indo-Chinese Cuisine.

It was my pleasure to bring these special recipes to the United States and share these exquisite meals with very special people. I am very honored to bring all the real tastes of ‘Awadh’ to this restaurant in Columbus. Please allow yourself to be enveloped with aromatic spices, pure meats without antibiotics, and homemade Naan and Lassi.

Our Mission

At TGIExpress, our mission is simple: enhance and educate the palate with the freshest ingredients and flavors, while surprising and exciting each guest with personal care and service.
